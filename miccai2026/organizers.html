STS 2026 : The 4th Semi-supervised Teeth Segmentation Challenge on Metal Artifact Reduction and Beyond

MICCAI 2026, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates

October 4-8, 2026

Challenge Organizers

STS 2026 brings together technical and clinical researchers working on artifact-resilient dental AI, CBCT analysis and digital dentistry. The organizing team listed below is adapted from the 2026 challenge proposal.

The challenge combines expertise from engineering, oral radiology, periodontology and clinical dental workflows to support a real-world benchmark for metal-artifact-aware segmentation and registration.

Clinical team responsibilities include selecting cases across mild, moderate and severe artifact levels, validating segmentation masks in artifact-obscured regions with a human-in-the-loop protocol, and assessing the clinical utility of IOS-CBCT registration accuracy.
