Challenge-Related Publications

STS 2026 will coordinate a challenge proceedings volume and a comprehensive journal paper covering the challenge design, dataset, submitted methods, results and artifact-severity analysis. Top-performing and innovative teams may be invited as co-authors according to contribution.

Related STS publications:
