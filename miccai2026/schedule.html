STS 2026 : The 4th Semi-supervised Teeth Segmentation Challenge on Metal Artifact Reduction and Beyond

MICCAI 2026, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates

October 4-8, 2026

Important Dates and Timeline

STS 2026 is planned as an online challenge hosted on Codabench, with the challenge community meeting during MICCAI 2026 in Abu Dhabi. The table below follows the timetable in the official 2026 challenge design document.

Date Milestone Notes
April 1, 2026 Registration opens and training data released Labeled and unlabeled training cases become available.
June 1, 2026 Validation data released The validation evaluation server opens for preliminary submissions.
July 15, 2026 Docker submission portal opens Teams can submit Docker containers for the final test set.
August 1, 2026 Final submission deadline Final test submissions close.
August 15, 2026 Results announcement and workshop invitations Top teams are announced and invited to the STS 2026 satellite event.
October 4 and 8, 2026 MICCAI 2026 satellite events Satellite event window for challenge-related activities.
October 5-7, 2026 MICCAI 2026 main conference Main conference dates in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.

Evaluation code and platform instructions will be published through the official STS 2026 GitHub repository and the Codabench competition platform.
